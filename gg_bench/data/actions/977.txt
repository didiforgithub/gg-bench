In the game **Command Clash**, the action space indices represent the following moves:
- **0: Charge** - Gain +1 CP, cannot exceed 5 CP.
- **1: Attack** - Spend 1 CP to deal 1 damage unless blocked.
- **2: Special Attack** - Spend 3 CP to deal 3 damage unless blocked.
- **3: Shield** - Spend 1 CP to block damage for the turn.
