In the `CustomEnv` gym environment, each action space index (0 to 8) corresponds to a specific cell in the 3x3 grid, represented as follows:

- **Index 0**: Top-left cell (0,0)
- **Index 1**: Top-middle cell (0,1)
- **Index 2**: Top-right cell (0,2)
- **Index 3**: Middle-left cell (1,0)
- **Index 4**: Center cell (1,1)
- **Index 5**: Middle-right cell (1,2)
- **Index 6**: Bottom-left cell (2,0)
- **Index 7**: Bottom-middle cell (2,1)
- **Index 8**: Bottom-right cell (2,2)

Players choose an action corresponding to the index of the cell they wish to move to or place their token.
