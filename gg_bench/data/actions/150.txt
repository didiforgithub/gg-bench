The action space consists of 6 discrete actions that map to game moves as follows:

- **Action 0:** Remove 1 stone from own pile (self-reduce and transfer to opponent).
- **Action 1:** Remove 2 stones from own pile (self-reduce and transfer to opponent).
- **Action 2:** Remove 3 stones from own pile (self-reduce and transfer to opponent).
- **Action 3:** Remove 1 stone from opponent's pile (attack opponent's pile and steal stones).
- **Action 4:** Remove 2 stones from opponent's pile (attack opponent's pile and steal stones).
- **Action 5:** Remove 3 stones from opponent's pile (attack opponent's pile and steal stones).
