In the action space mapping for the Grid Snatchers game, there are 8 possible actions, each corresponding to a combination of direction and distance. The mapping is as follows: 

- `0`: Move up 1 space.
- `1`: Move up 2 spaces.
- `2`: Move down 1 space.
- `3`: Move down 2 spaces.
- `4`: Move left 1 space.
- `5`: Move left 2 spaces.
- `6`: Move right 1 space.
- `7`: Move right 2 spaces.

Each action index corresponds to one of these combinations, allowing players to select how their token moves on their turn.
