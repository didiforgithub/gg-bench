In the game, the action space indices are mapped to specific moves for each player. For Player 1, who moves rightward on the number line, the actions are: 

- 0: Move forward 1 position (+1)
- 1: Move forward 2 positions (+2)
- 2: Move backward 1 position (-1)
- 3: Move backward 2 positions (-2)

For Player 2, who moves leftward, the actions are:

- 0: Move forward 1 position (-1, towards Player 1's base)
- 1: Move forward 2 positions (-2)
- 2: Move backward 1 position (+1, towards their own base)
- 3: Move backward 2 positions (+2)
