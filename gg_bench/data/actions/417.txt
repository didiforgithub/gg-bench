In the "Numerical Nexus" gym environment, the action space indices correspond to the following player moves:

- **0:** Perform "+1" addition on the player's score.
- **1:** Perform "+3" addition on the player's score.
- **2:** Perform "+5" addition on the player's score.
- **3:** Multiply the player's score by "×2".
- **4:** Multiply the player's score by "×3".
- **5:** Use the reset operation to set the player's score to 0.
- **6:** Use the reset operation to set the opponent's score to 0.

Each action corresponds to executing one of the game's available operations when managing the player's score or countering the opponent's progress.
