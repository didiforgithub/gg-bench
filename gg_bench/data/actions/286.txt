The action space indices in the game map to the moves as follows:

- **0**: Firebolt - Deals 1 damage to the opponent.
- **1**: Lightning Strike - Deals 2 damage to the opponent.
- **2**: Dark Pact - Deals 3 damage to the opponent, costs 1 HP from the caster.
- **3**: Shield - Activates a shield to block the next incoming spell.
- **4**: Healing Touch - Restores 2 HP to the caster (up to a maximum of 10 HP).
