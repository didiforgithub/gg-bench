The action space in the game is mapped to the following moves:

- Index 0 corresponds to the `charge` action, which increases a player's energy points (EP) by 1 without any cost.
- Index 1 corresponds to the `shield` action, which activates a shield that blocks the next incoming attack at the cost of 1 EP.
- Index 2 corresponds to the `attack` action, which deals 2 damage to the opponent's crystal at the cost of 2 EP.
