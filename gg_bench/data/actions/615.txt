In the Dueling Wizards game environment, the action space indices map to the following spell moves:

- **0**: Magic Missile (Unblockable, deals 1 damage)
- **1**: Fireball (Blockable, deals 2 damage)
- **2**: Shield (Activates blocking for the next turn)
- **3**: Heal (Restores 2 HP, max HP is 10)
- **4**: Lightning Strike (Blockable, deals 4 damage if not blocked)
