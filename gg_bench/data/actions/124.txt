In the action space for this game, the indices map to moves as follows:

- **0-4**: Each index corresponds to playing a card from the player's hand at the respective position during the attack or defense phase. Index 0 plays the first card in hand, index 1 plays the second card, and so on.
- **5**: Indicates the decision to choose not to defend during the defense phase, resulting in taking full damage from the opponent's attack.
