In the defined gym environment for Grid Runner, the action space consists of 5 discrete indices, each corresponding to a specific move for a player's token:

- **Index 0**: Move Forward (Player 1 moves down; Player 2 moves up).
- **Index 1**: Move Left (decrease the column number).
- **Index 2**: Move Right (increase the column number).
- **Index 3**: Diagonal Forward Left (move diagonally forward and left).
- **Index 4**: Diagonal Forward Right (move diagonally forward and right).
