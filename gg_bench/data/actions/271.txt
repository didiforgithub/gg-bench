The action space indices are mapped to the game moves as follows:

- Indices 0-4: Play a Charge Card, adding 1 to 5 charge points to your total, respectively.
- Index 5: Play a Block Card to prevent your opponent from gaining charge points on their next turn.
- Index 6: Play a Steal Card to take the charge points your opponent gained during their last turn.
- Index 7: Forfeit your turn to refresh all used Charge Cards.
