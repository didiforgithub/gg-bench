The action space indices (0 to 24) map to the grid coordinates in a row-major order on the 5x5 grid:

- Index 0 corresponds to coordinate A1
- Index 1 corresponds to coordinate A2
- ...
- Index 4 corresponds to coordinate A5
- Index 5 corresponds to coordinate B1
- ...
- Index 20 corresponds to coordinate E1
- ...
- Index 24 corresponds to coordinate E5

Each index determines the grid position a player chooses to scan in their turn.
