The action space indices map to the game moves as follows:

- **0**: Move forward by 1 position.
- **1**: Move forward by 2 positions.
- **2**: Move forward by 3 positions.
- **3**: Rest (only when at player's base, position 0).
- **4**: Play battle card 1.
- **5**: Play battle card 2.
- **6**: Play battle card 3.
- **7**: Play battle card 4.
- **8**: Play battle card 5.
