In the given game environment, the action space indices are mapped to moves on the number line as follows:

- **Action 0**: Move the token 1 step to the left.
- **Action 1**: Move the token 1 step to the right.
- **Action 2**: Move the token 2 steps to the left.
- **Action 3**: Move the token 2 steps to the right.

Each action corresponds to a specific movement of the token either left or right by 1 or 2 positions.
