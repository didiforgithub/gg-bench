In the game, the action space indices are mapped to moves as follows:
- **Index 0**: Move the token -2 positions (backward 2 spaces).
- **Index 1**: Move the token -1 position (backward 1 space).
- **Index 2**: Move the token +1 position (forward 1 space).
- **Index 3**: Move the token +2 positions (forward 2 spaces).
